how to reach out to someone struggling

how to help someone struggling with mental health:

  • Listen and validate their feelings, providing emotional support goes a long way.

  • Offer them resources and helplines. Ensure them that it is always okay to reach out for help

  • Educate yourself more on mental health and mental illnesses

  • Respect their privacy but also be sure check up on them and ask how they are doing

  • If you feel like you are not equip to handle a situation, reach out to a trusted adult to help

  • Don't immediately try to "solve" their problems, and rather ask how you can support them

where to go if you're struggling:

  • Call Kids Help Phone at 1-800-668-6868

  • Visit the Durham Distress Centre or call 905-430-2522

  • Talk to someone in guidance or a teacher

  • Visit prevnet.ca for help with bullying or other safety issues

  • Visit PFLAG Durham

  • Visit Durham Couselling Walk-In Clinic
